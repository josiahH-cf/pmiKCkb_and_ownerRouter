Live today

Already running in your app. Full detail is in the app guide.

  • Ask anything and get answers grounded in your approved knowledge, with sources cited, across every area of your operation.
  • The guided lease-renewal desk: triage, a step-by-step flow per lease, data-conflict catching, and renewal drafts created for a person to send.
  • Comp-informed rent suggestion: the app can suggest a renewal rent from the comps you record, for an Admin to approve.
  • The approval queue: one decision inbox with required reasons, separation of duties, and a permanent record.
  • Maintenance: capture a request, build the work order, run the ticket through its life, and draft the owner notice.
  • Governed communications: drafting and thread summaries, plus one carefully confirmed send and no blast button anywhere.
  • Notifications, roles and audit logs, feedback, and a real spending cap.

Coming next

Built and waiting on one small step each. The label shows what unlocks it.

Automatic rent comparables

Ready when you provide a key

Pull comparable local listings automatically, so the market range fills in for you instead of being entered by hand.

Unlocks with: a RentCast data key (free tier).

Save approved values back to RentVine

Ready when RentVine provides the write step

Once you approve a renewal value, write it back into RentVine so you do not re-key it. Every write shows a preview, waits for your confirmation, and can be undone.

Unlocks with: the documented RentVine write endpoint.

E-signature for renewals

Ready when you register a Dotloop app

Send renewal documents for signature through Dotloop and get a signal back when they are signed.

Unlocks with: a Dotloop application you register and authorize.

LeadSimple connector

Ready when you provide a key

Connect the workflows that use LeadSimple, behind the same preview-and-confirm safeguards.

Unlocks with: a LeadSimple API key and a short confirmation of which endpoints to use.

A few decisions to finalize together

Each has a recommended default, so you can say "go with the default" or steer it.

Keep the practice mode, or move to a live-only app?

The app keeps a safe practice mode and clear "test" labels so a mistaken click can never send something real.

Recommended: keep the practice mode. If you want a live-only version, we build it as its own reviewed project.

Which tenant is the main contact?

Renewal notices go to the first tenant on the lease, with co-tenants copied.

Recommended: keep addressing all tenants. We just need Dan to confirm the first tenant is the right lead, or tell us the rule.

Confirm the RentVine field names

The renewal desk reads the recipient and rent from specific RentVine fields.

Recommended: Dan confirms the field names against a couple of real leases before the first live draft.

New-staff access

Today a new sign-in can see all areas. Your preference is that new people see nothing until an Admin grants access.

Recommended: build it so existing people keep their access and only brand-new accounts start empty. We just need your go-ahead.

One-time setup and your branding

A short list of one-time steps in your Google Cloud console gets the app fully production-ready: turning on data backups before real client data lands, one or two safety settings, and the connection keys listed above. Separately, sending us your official PMI brand files (logo, colors, and fonts) lets us replace the current clean default styling with your real brand.

Every one of these, with the exact step and where to do it, is written up in the companion unblock guide.
