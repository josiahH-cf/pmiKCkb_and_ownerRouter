# Move-In Sanitized Call Notes

Source status: Transcript-derived
Space: Move-In
Sensitivity: Low

This sanitized summary is based on local PMI KC workflow call notes. It contains no
tenant names, owner names, property addresses, lease documents, payment amounts, animal
records, utility account details, or inspection records.

## Supported Workflow Facts

- Move-ins are tracked in a manual Google Sheet.
- The checklist includes processing fee status, animal registration, utility proof,
  document signing, certified funds, Z-inspection link, owner inspection notes, lawn
  care, key handoff, welcome letter, review request, listing disablement, and lockbox
  pickup.
- The team needs visibility into which move-in steps are done and which are still
  missing.

## Demo Use

The KB may explain the move-in checklist shape and why missing prerequisites should
become visible placeholders. It should not answer exact payment, lease, inspection, or
exception rules without approved sources.

## Do Not Use This Source To

- Verify tenant payment or utility account status.
- Update RentVine, Z-inspection, Sheets, Gmail, or listing platforms.
- Produce final move-in instructions.
