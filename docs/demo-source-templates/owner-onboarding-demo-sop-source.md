# Owner Onboarding Demo SOP Source

Source status: Approved demo seed
Space: Owner Onboarding
Sensitivity: Low

This is safe demo content for proving the PMI KC KB live Ask path. It contains no real
owner, tenant, address, lease, deposit, account, or personally identifying data.

## Workflow

1. Check the onboarding tracker before treating setup details as known.
2. Confirm agreement status, utilities, insurance, keys or locks, filters, and lawn
   care.
3. Confirm lease takeover, tenant contact, and deposit tracking when applicable.
4. Create placeholders for missing setup ownership, timing, or system-of-record facts.

## Handling Rules

- Do not mark a property as fully ready unless required setup facts are documented.
- Do not invent utility, insurance, key, lock, tenant, or deposit details.
- Do not update RentVine, onboarding sheets, Gmail, Drive, or any system of record.
- Use placeholders when responsibility, timing, or required checklist details are
  unclear.

## Escalation

Escalate missing onboarding ownership, existing-tenant transition questions, and any
system-of-record updates to the process owner.

## Good Ask Questions For Smoke Testing

- What details does the team track during owner onboarding?
- What onboarding gaps should become placeholders?
- Can the KB update the onboarding sheet or RentVine?
