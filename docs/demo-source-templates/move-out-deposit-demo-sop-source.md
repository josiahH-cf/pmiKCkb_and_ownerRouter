# Move-Out And Deposit Disposition Demo SOP Source

Source status: Approved demo seed
Space: Move-Out + Deposit Disposition
Sensitivity: Low

This is safe demo content for proving the PMI KC KB live Ask path. It contains no real
owner, tenant, address, charge, ledger, bank, inspection record, or personally
identifying data.

## Workflow

1. Confirm whether move-out notice was received or sent.
2. Check the move-out tracker for tenant instructions, owner utility reminders, locks,
   inspection status, and vendor-bid status.
3. Review inspection and repair context before owner-facing commitments.
4. Escalate deposit-sensitive decisions and legal wording instead of guessing.

## Handling Rules

- Do not decide deposit disposition or tenant charges from the KB.
- Do not invent legal deadlines, notice language, fees, or exception rules.
- If inspection, vendor, utility, or RentVine close-out details are missing, create a
  placeholder before drafting a final tenant-facing answer.
- The KB does not update RentVine, accounting records, inspection systems, Gmail, or
  any system of record.

## Escalation

Escalate deposit disposition, legal wording, charge decisions, and missing inspection
or vendor facts to the process owner before presenting a final answer.

## Good Ask Questions For Smoke Testing

- What has to happen after a tenant gives move-out notice?
- Why is move-out work deadline-sensitive?
- Can the KB decide deposit disposition by itself?
