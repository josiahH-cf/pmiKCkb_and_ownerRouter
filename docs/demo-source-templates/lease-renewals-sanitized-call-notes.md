# Lease Renewals Sanitized Call Notes

Source status: Transcript-derived
Space: Lease Renewals
Sensitivity: Low

This review-required source summarizes PMI KC's lease renewal handoff: the team checks
Rentvine and the renewal spreadsheet, contacts the property owner before making
tenant-facing renewal commitments, waits for documented owner direction on renewal
terms, hands agreed renewal details into DotLoop, and uses Rentvine for tenant
signature reminders. It gives the live Ask demo real PMI KC process context without

## Renewal Workflow Facts

- Where the team checks current lease status: Rentvine, with the renewal tracking
  spreadsheet used as the at-a-glance work queue.
- Where the team checks renewal status: the renewal tracking spreadsheet, Rentvine,
  DotLoop signature status, and the relevant owner or tenant communication thread.
- Who owns the renewal decision: the owner decides whether to renew, raise rent, keep
  terms, ask for a recommendation, or not renew; Dan is copied/reviewed on owner-facing
  renewal outreach.
- What must be confirmed before owner-facing commitments: current rent, property facts,
  comp context from Zillow and the PMI Free Rental Analysis tool, and whether the
  recommendation is supported by documented sources.
- What must be confirmed before tenant-facing commitments: owner direction, renewal or
  non-renewal decision, proposed rent/term details, and whether DotLoop documents are
  ready for signature.

## First-Pass Handoff Flow

1. Review the renewal spreadsheet to identify upcoming lease expirations.
2. Check Rentvine for current rent, property facts, lease status, owner information,
   and tenant communication context.
3. Pull market context from Zillow and the PMI Free Rental Analysis tool when preparing
   owner-facing pricing context.
4. Draft or send owner outreach through Gmail using documented facts only; include Dan
   when the current process calls for his review.
5. Record the owner response in the renewal tracking workflow.
6. If the owner chooses renewal, prepare tenant-facing outreach in Rentvine after the
   owner decision and relevant terms are known.
7. If the tenant agrees, hand the needed details to the teammate preparing renewal
   documents in DotLoop.
8. After DotLoop sends documents, use Rentvine to remind tenants that signatures are
   needed.
9. Continue follow-up until documents are signed or the issue needs escalation.

## Owner Approval Rules

- Owner approval is required when: deciding whether to renew, changing rent, setting
  renewal terms, or choosing not to renew.
- Owner approval is not required when: not confirmed by the source notes.
- Exceptions that should be escalated: missing comps, conflicting rent data, owner
  non-response near a deadline, owner requests for recommendations, non-renewal
  decisions, or any legal/notice uncertainty.
