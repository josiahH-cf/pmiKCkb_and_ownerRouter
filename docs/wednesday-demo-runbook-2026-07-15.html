The 12 to 15 minute walkthrough

1

Review a renewal decision on a phone

  1. Open the safe sample run: /lease-renewal/runs/sim-renewal-001.
  2. Confirm Decide one at a time is selected.
  3. Point out the N-of-M progress, source comparison, suggested source, and write-back guardrail.
  4. Tap Next once. The card should advance without horizontal page scrolling.
  5. Explain that High or Blocked items require a written reason before resolution.

Demo-safe stopping point: do not press Resolve or authorize a write-back unless the meeting explicitly includes an approved app-plane test decision. Nothing on this screen writes to RentVine or the operating Sheet.

2

Show how Console and Notifications guide the next action

  1. Open /ask.
  2. Show the three attention cards: decisions, connections, and space coverage.
  3. Note the decision count, then open Notifications and choose the full view.
  4. Confirm the Needs your decision count matches Console.
  5. Show the separate recent-activity log and setup lists. Do not promise email notifications.

Counts can change. The check is that Console and Notifications agree, not that a fixed number appears. The July 13 verification showed 8 in both places.

3

Prepare a draft and summarize a pasted thread in Gmail Hub

  1. Open /gmail-hub.
  2. Confirm the signed-in pmikcmetro.com mailbox is connected. Use Start or renew push watch when the watch needs renewal.
  3. In Simulated email chain, point out the Browser only badge, then press Add simulated reply.
  4. Confirm the counter changes from 1 message to 2 messages in one simulated thread. Nothing was delivered or saved; refresh resets the fixture.
  5. In Anticipatory draft, choose Scheduling acknowledgement - Approved.
  6. Use the safe inputs below, then press Compose draft.
Sender: dan@example.com
Category: Scheduling
Subject: Re: Wednesday PMI KC demo walkthrough
Facts to verify:
Wednesday demo walkthrough requested
Agenda is five steps
No automatic send or system-of-record writes
  • Draft appears with the review-before-sending banner.
  • Each supplied fact appears as a Needs Verification line.
  • No Send control appears.

Next, paste a short sanitized version of the project-update email into Thread summary and press Summarize thread. Confirm Summary, Waiting on, and Suggested next action appear.

4

Review upcoming work before it becomes urgent

  1. Return to Console and scroll to Anticipated work.
  2. Show renewal-window and tenant-notice counts plus any all-clear or waiting-on-signal lanes.
  3. Read the caption: it is computed on request, never scheduled, and never sends.
  4. Use the linked Space to explain the next step without starting a run.

Starting a test run creates an app-owned workflow record. It still does not send or update a system of record, but skip the click unless the meeting calls for that app-plane write.

5

Walk through maintenance intake to assignment

  1. Open /maintenance.
  2. Type Demo only: kitchen faucet is dripping slowly.
  3. Leave the unit empty and press Build work-order draft.
  4. Show the inferred Plumbing trade, owner-notice draft, vendor suggestion, and unmatched-unit blocker.
  5. Confirm Create ticket is disabled until a verified unit is selected.
  6. In the Ticket queue, show the status choices and the Dan/Josiah assignee choices.

For a full create-and-assign demonstration, preselect a client-approved unit. Creating or assigning a ticket writes only to the app's Firestore collections, not RentVine. Get explicit meeting approval before creating the test record, then close it with a clear demo reason.

Optional grounded Ask smoke

  1. Open Console and leave Process on Just ask (no process).
  2. Ask: What is the lease renewal workflow?
  3. Press Get answer.
  4. Confirm the answer says Verified Source and lists approved-source citations.

This invokes the production Flash model and retrieval path. Run it once, not repeatedly. Selecting a process changes the button to start a test run, so use Just ask for a read-only smoke.
